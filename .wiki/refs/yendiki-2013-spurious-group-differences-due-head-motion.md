# A. Yendiki, Kami Koldewyn, Sita Kakunoori, N. Kanwisher, B. Fischl (2013)

*Spurious group differences due to head motion in a diffusion MRI study* — NeuroImage.

## Claim

It is shown that group Differences in head motion can induce group differences in DW-MRI measures, and that this is the case even when comparing groups that include control subjects only, where no anisotropy or diffusivity differences are expected.
