# J. Andersson, S. Sotiropoulos (2015)

*Non-parametric representation and prediction of single- and multi-shell diffusion-weighted MRI data using Gaussian processes* — NeuroImage.

## Claim

A novel way to represent and make predictions about diffusion MRI data is described, based on a Gaussian process on one or several spheres similar to the Geostatistical method of “Kriging”.
