# Quinten Collier, J. Veraart, B. Jeurissen, A. J. den Dekker, Jan Sijbers (2015)

*Iterative reweighted linear least squares for accurate, fast, and robust estimation of diffusion magnetic resonance parameters* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

This work states that diffusion‐weighted magnetic resonance imaging suffers from physiological noise, such as artifacts caused by motion or system instabilities, and there is a need for robust diffusion parameter estimation techniques.
