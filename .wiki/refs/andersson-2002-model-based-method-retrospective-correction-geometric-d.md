# J. Andersson, S. Skare (2002)

*A Model-Based Method for Retrospective Correction of Geometric Distortions in Diffusion-Weighted EPI* — NeuroImage.

## Claim

This paper suggests and implements a method for retrospective correction of geometric distortion firmly based on a physical model for the diffusion-weighted images and suggests models for how distortions vary with slice position and gradient direction that allow us to substantially reduce the dimensionality of the parameter space.
