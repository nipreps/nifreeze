*On the effects of gating in diffusion imaging of the brain using single shot EPI.* — Magnetic Resonance Imaging.

## Claim

The necessity of performing cardiac gating with single shot echo planar imaging with F statistics of the variance in DWI data has been explored and a minimum trigger delay may be the best choice in terms of SNR per unit of experiment time.
