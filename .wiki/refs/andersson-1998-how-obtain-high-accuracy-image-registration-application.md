# J. Andersson (1998)

*How to obtain high-accuracy image registration: application to movement correction of dynamic positron emission tomography data* — European Journal of Nuclear Medicine.

## Claim

It is shown that most existing algorithms introduce a bias, the size of which depends on the pixel size and the signal-to-noise ratio of the data, and a sequential registration of each frame to its predecessor may be used, provided the registration algorithm is completely free of bias.
