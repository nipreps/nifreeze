# M. Genton (2002)

*Classes of Kernels for Machine Learning: A Statistics Perspective* — Journal of machine learning research.

## Claim

The spectral representation of the various classes of kernels is described and a discussion on the characterization of nonlinear maps that reduce nonstationary kernels to either stationarity or local stationarity is discussed.
