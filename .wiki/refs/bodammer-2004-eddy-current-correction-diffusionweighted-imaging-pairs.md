# N. Bodammer, J. Kaufmann, M. Kanowski, C. Tempelmann (2004)

*Eddy current correction in diffusion‐weighted imaging using pairs of images acquired with opposite diffusion gradient polarity* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

With phantom data it is shown that the theoretically expected symmetry of distortions is preserved in the images to a very high degree, demonstrating the practicality of the new postprocessing correction scheme.
