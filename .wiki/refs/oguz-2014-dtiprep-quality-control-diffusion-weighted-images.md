*DTIPrep: quality control of diffusion-weighted images* — Front. Neuroinform..

## Claim

This manuscript presents the open-source software, DTIPrep, as a unified, user friendly platform for thorough QC of dMRI data, and focuses on newly added capabilities related to directional artifacts and bias analysis.
