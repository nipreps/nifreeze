*Pushing spatial and temporal resolution for functional and diffusion MRI in the Human Connectome Project* — NeuroImage.

## Claim

Technical improvements and optimization of these methods as well as instrumental choices that impact speed of acquisition of fMRI and dMRI images at 3 Tesla are described, leading to whole brain coverage with 2 mm isotropic resolution fMRI data for tractography analysis with three-fold reduction in total data acquisition time.
