# M. Unser, A. Aldroubi, M. Eden (1993)

*B-SPLINE SIGNAL PROCESSING: PART II-EFFICIENT DESIGN AND APPLICATIONS*.

## Claim

This paper describes a class of recursive filtering algorithms for the efficient implementation of B-spline inter- polation and approximation techniques and shows how to con- struct a cubic spline image pyramid that minimizes the loss of information in passage from one resolution level to the next.
