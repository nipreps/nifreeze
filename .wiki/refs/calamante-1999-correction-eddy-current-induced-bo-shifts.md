# F. Calamante, D. Porter, D. Gadian, A. Connelly (1999)

*Correction for eddy current induced Bo shifts in diffusion‐weighted echo‐planar imaging* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

An on‐line correction method is described that can provide ADC maps that are free of misregistration artifacts, without the need for off‐line postprocessing, and phantom and human images that demonstrate the validity of the technique are presented.
