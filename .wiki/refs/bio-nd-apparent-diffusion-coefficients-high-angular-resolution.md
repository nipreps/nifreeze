# Thème Bio, Maxime Descoteaux, E. Angelino, S. Fitzgibbons, R. Deriche, Septembre (nd)

*Apparent Diffusion Coefﬁcients from High Angular Resolution Diffusion Images: Estimation and Applications*.

## Claim

This paper presents an extensive study of high order models for apparent diffusion coefﬁcient estimation and illustrates some of their applications and develops the appropriate mathematical tools to work on noisy HARDI data.
