# Zhenyu Zhou, Wei Liu, J. Cui, Xunheng Wang, Diana Arias, Ying Wen, R. Bansal, Xuejun Hao, Zhishun Wang, B. Peterson, Dongrong Xu (2011)

*Automated artifact detection and removal for improved tensor estimation in motion-corrupted DTI data sets using the combination of local binary patterns and 2D partial least squares.* — Magnetic Resonance Imaging.

## Claim

It is shown that this method can effectively extract local features from brain images obtained from a large sample of human infants to identify images that are outliers in their textural features, permitting their exclusion from further processing when estimating tensors using the DWIs.
