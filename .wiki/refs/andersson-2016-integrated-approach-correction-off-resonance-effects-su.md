*An integrated approach to correction for off-resonance effects and subject movement in diffusion MR imaging* — NeuroImage.

## Claim

The method is based on registering the individual volumes to a model free prediction of what each volume should look like, thereby enabling its use on high b-value data where the contrast is vastly different in different volumes.
