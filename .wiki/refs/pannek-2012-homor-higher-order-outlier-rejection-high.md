*HOMOR: Higher Order Model Outlier Rejection for high b-value MR diffusion data* — NeuroImage.

## Claim

HOMOR (higher order model outlier rejection) was developed to overcome this limitation and was found to identify a decreased number of outlier voxels compared to RESTORE primarily within areas of crossing, bending and fanning fibres.
