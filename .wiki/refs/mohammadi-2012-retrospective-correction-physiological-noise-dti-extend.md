# S. Mohammadi, C. Hutton, Z. Nagy, O. Josephs, N. Weiskopf (2012)

*Retrospective Correction of Physiological Noise in DTI Using an Extended Tensor Model and Peripheral Measurements* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

An easy‐to‐implement extension of the original diffusion Tensor model to account for physiological noise in diffusion tensor imaging using measures of peripheral physiology (pulse and respiration), the so‐called extended tensor model is developed.
