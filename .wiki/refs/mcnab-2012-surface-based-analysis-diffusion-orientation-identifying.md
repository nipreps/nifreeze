# J. McNab, J. Polimeni, Ruopeng Wang, J. Augustinack, K. Fujimoto, A. Stevens, T. Janssens, R. Farivar, R. Folkerth, W. Vanduffel, L. Wald (2012)

*Surface Based Analysis of Diffusion Orientation for Identifying Architectonic Domains in the In Vivo Human Cortex* — NeuroImage.

## Claim

The study of cortical diffusion anisotropy is extended by relating it to the local coordinate system of the folded cerebral cortex by using 1mm and sub-millimeter isotropic resolution diffusion imaging to perform a laminar analysis of the principal diffusion orientation, fractional anisotropy, mean diffusivity and partial volume effects.
