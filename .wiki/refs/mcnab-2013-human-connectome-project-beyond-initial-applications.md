# J. McNab, B. Edlow, T. Witzel, S. Huang, H. Bhat, K. Heberlein, T. Feiweier, Kecheng Liu, B. Keil, J. Cohen-Adad, M. Tisdall, R. Folkerth, H. Kinney, L. Wald (2013)

*The Human Connectome Project and Beyond: Initial Applications of 300 mT/m Gradients* — NeuroImage.

## Claim

It is shown that the improved sensitivity and diffusion-resolution provided by the gradients is rapidly enabling human applications of techniques that were previously possible only for in vitro and animal models on small-bore scanners, thereby creating novel opportunities to map the microstructure of the human brain in health and disease.
