# M. Graham, I. Drobnjak, Hui Zhang (2015)

*A Simulation Framework for Quantitative Validation of Artefact Correction in Diffusion MRI* — Information Processing in Medical Imaging.

## Claim

A simulation framework is demonstrated that enables the direct and quantitative comparison of post-processing methods for diffusion weighted magnetic resonance (DW-MR) images, enabling objective comparisons of existing and future methods.
