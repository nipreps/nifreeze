# P. Storey, F. Frigo, R. S. Hinks, B. Mock, B. Collick, Nicole M. Baker, J. Marmurek, S. Graham (2007)

*Partial k‐space reconstruction in single‐shot diffusion‐weighted echo‐planar imaging* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

An adaptive version of the homodyne algorithm is presented that detects the location of the echo in k‐space and adjusts the center and width of the Homodyne filters accordingly, and produces artifact‐free images when the echo is shifted into the high positive k‐ space range.
