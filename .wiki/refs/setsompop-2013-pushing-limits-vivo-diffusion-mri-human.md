*Pushing the limits of in vivo diffusion MRI for the Human Connectome Project* — NeuroImage.

## Claim

The Human Connectome Project is to address limiting factors by re-engineering the scanner from the ground up to optimize the high b-value, high angular resolution diffusion imaging needed for sensitive and accurate mapping of the brain's structural connections by implementing a novel 4-port drive geometry and optimizing size and linearity for the brain.
