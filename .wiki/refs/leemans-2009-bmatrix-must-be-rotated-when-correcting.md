*The B‐matrix must be rotated when correcting for subject motion in DTI data* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

A systematic study to investigate the effect of neglecting to reorient the B‐matrix on DTI data during motion correction is presented and the consequences for diffusion fiber tractography are discussed.
