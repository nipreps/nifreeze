# P. Basser (2009)

*Diffusion MRI: From Quantitative Measurement to In vivo Neuroanatomy*.

## Claim

The basic principles governing the diffusion phenomenon and its measurement using magnetic resonance (MR) are reviewed and the concepts of the apparent diffusion coefficient and of the diffusion propagator as well as their MR measurements are introduced from basic principles.
