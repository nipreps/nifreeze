# B. Wilm, C. Barmet, M. Pavan, K. Pruessmann (2011)

*Higher order reconstruction for MRI in the presence of spatiotemporal field perturbations* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

This work describes an alternative approach in which the entire field evolution, including higher order effects, is accounted for by viewing image reconstruction as a generic inverse problem and demonstrates that the resulting geometric consistency permits straightforward tensor analysis without coregistration.
