# I. Drobnjak, G. Pell, M. Jenkinson (2010)

*Simulating the effects of time-varying magnetic fields with a realistic simulated scanner.* — Magnetic Resonance Imaging.

## Claim

An existing MR simulator is extended with a model for changing magnetic fields at a very high-resolution time-scale and the observation that the current-induced effects included ringing in the image, which extended away from the conductor, primarily in the phase-encode direction was observed.
