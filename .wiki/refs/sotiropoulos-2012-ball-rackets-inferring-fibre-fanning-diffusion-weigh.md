# S. Sotiropoulos, Timothy Edward John Behrens, S. Jbabdi (2012)

*Ball and Rackets: Inferring Fibre Fanning from Diffusion-weighted MRI* — NeuroImage.

## Claim

This study focuses on fibre orientation dispersion induced by within-voxel fanning, using a multi- compartment, model-based approach to estimate fibre dispersion and performs a qualitative comparison between the model and five popular non-parametric techniques that are based on orientation distribution functions (ODFs).
