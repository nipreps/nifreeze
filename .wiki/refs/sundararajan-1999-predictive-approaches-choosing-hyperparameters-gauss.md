# S. Sundararajan, S. Keerthi (1999)

*Predictive Approaches for Choosing Hyperparameters in Gaussian Processes* — Neural Computation.

## Claim

Predictive approaches based on Geisser's predictive sample reuse (PSR) methodology and the related Stone's cross-validation (CV) methodology are proposed and investigated and Experimental results show that these approaches are strongly competitive with the existing approaches.
