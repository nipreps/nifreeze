# M. Graham, I. Drobnjak, Hui Zhang (2016)

*Realistic simulation of artefacts in diffusion MRI for validating post-processing correction techniques* — NeuroImage.

## Claim

A simulation framework that enables the direct and quantitative comparison of post-processing methods for diffusion weighted magnetic resonance (DW-MR) images is demonstrated, demonstrating quantitatively that using eddy_correct leads to significant errors in the corrected data, whilst eddy is able to provide much improved correction.
