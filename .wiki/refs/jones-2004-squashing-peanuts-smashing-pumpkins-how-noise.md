# Derek K. Jones, P. Basser (2004)

*“Squashing peanuts and smashing pumpkins”: How noise distorts diffusion‐weighted MR data* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

Several new artifacts that can be explained by considering how background noise affects the peanut‐shaped angular apparent diffusion coefficient (ADC) profile are reported, including an orientationally dependent deviation from Gaussian behavior of the ADC profile, an underestimation of indices of diffusion anisotropy, and a correlation between estimates of mean diffusivity and diffusion anIsotropy.
