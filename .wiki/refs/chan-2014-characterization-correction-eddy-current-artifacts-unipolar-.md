# R. Chan, Constantin von Deuster, D. Giese, C. Stoeck, J. Harmer, Andy Aitken, D. Atkinson, S. Kozerke (2014)

*Characterization and correction of eddy-current artifacts in unipolar and bipolar diffusion sequences using magnetic field monitoring.* — Journal of magnetic resonance (San Diego, Calif. 1997 : Print).

## Claim

Results demonstrate the need for second-order correction in unipolar encoding schemes but also show that bipolar sequences benefit from second- order reconstruction to correct for incomplete intrinsic cancellation of eddy-currents.
