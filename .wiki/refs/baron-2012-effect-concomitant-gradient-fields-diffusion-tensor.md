# C. Baron, R. Lebel, A. Wilman, C. Beaulieu (2012)

*The effect of concomitant gradient fields on diffusion tensor imaging* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

Bias in apparent diffusion coefficient values was experimentally found to worsen with distance from isocenter and with increasing duration of gradient asymmetry in both a phantom and in the brain.
