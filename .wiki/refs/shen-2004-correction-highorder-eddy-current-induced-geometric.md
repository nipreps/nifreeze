# Yuji Shen, D. Larkman, S. Counsell, Ida M. Pu, David Edwards, J. Hajnal (2004)

*Correction of high‐order eddy current induced geometric distortion in diffusion‐weighted echo‐planar images* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

This study demonstrates a method that can correct for higher‐order errors by collecting pairs of images with diffusion sensitizing gradients reversed that has been tested by simulation, on phantoms, on adult volunteers, and on neonatal brain examinations.
