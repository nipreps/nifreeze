# M. Unser, A. Aldroubi, M. Eden (1993)

*B-spline signal processing. II. Efficiency design and applications* — IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing.

## Claim

A class of recursive filtering algorithms for the efficient implementation of B-spline interpolation and approximation techniques is described and it is shown how to construct a cubic spline image pyramid that minimizes the loss of information in passage from one resolution level to the next.
