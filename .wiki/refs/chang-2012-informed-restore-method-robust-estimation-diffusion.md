# Lin-Ching Chang, L. Walker, C. Pierpaoli (2012)

*Informed RESTORE: A Method for Robust Estimation of Diffusion Tensor from Low Redundancy Datasets in the Presence of Physiological Noise Artifacts* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

This article introduces a method, called informed RESTORE, designed to remove physiological noise artifacts in datasets acquired with low redundancy—a condition in which the original RESTORE algorithm may converge to an incorrect solution.
