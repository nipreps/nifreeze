# R. Price, M. Kadbi, Joshua Kim, J. Balter, I. Chetty, C. Glide-Hurst (2015)

*Technical Note: Characterization and correction of gradient nonlinearity induced distortion on a 1.0 T open bore MR-SIM.* — Medical Physics (Lancaster).

## Claim

Inherent distortion due to gradient nonlinearity was found to be non-negligible even with vendor corrections applied, and further corrections are required to obtain 1 mm accuracy for large FOVs.
