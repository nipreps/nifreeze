# J. Andersson, S. Sotiropoulos (2013)

*865 A Gaussian Process based method for detecting and correcting dropout in diffusion imaging*.

## Claim

This work has recently developed a method (EDDY) for simultaneously correcting for eddy current-induced distortions and subject movements based on comparing a Gaussian Process based prediction of the data with the observed data in the native scanner space.

Cited by [[andersson-2015-gp-dmri]], [[andersson-2016-integrated-eddy]] as part of nifreeze's theoretical foundation (GP-based prediction / integrated distortion-and-motion correction / outlier replacement). Verified against Semantic Scholar metadata; not yet read in full.
