# E. Caruyer, C. Lenglet, Guillermo Sapiro, R. Deriche (2013)

*Design of multishell sampling schemes with uniform coverage in diffusion MRI* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

In diffusion MRI, a technique known as diffusion spectrum imaging reconstructs the propagator with a discrete Fourier transform, from a Cartesian sampling of the diffusion signal, providing high angular resolution diffusion imaging.
