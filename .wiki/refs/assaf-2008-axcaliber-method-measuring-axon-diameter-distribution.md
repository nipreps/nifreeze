# Y. Assaf, T. Blumenfeld-Katzir, Y. Yovel, P. Basser (2008)

*Axcaliber: A method for measuring axon diameter distribution from diffusion MRI* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

A new NMR‐based approach that employs a model of water diffusion within “restricted” cylindrical axons to estimate their diameter distribution within a nerve bundle is proposed and validated by comparing the diameter distributions measured using the NMR and histological techniques on sciatic and optic nerve tissue specimens.
