# Dan Xu, J. K. Maier, K. King, B. Collick, Gaohong Wu, R. Peters, R. S. Hinks (2013)

*Prospective and retrospective high order eddy current mitigation for diffusion weighted echo planar imaging* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

The proposed method is aimed at reducing eddy current (EC) induced distortion in diffusion weighted echo planar imaging, without the need to perform further image coregistration between diffusion weighted and T2 images.
