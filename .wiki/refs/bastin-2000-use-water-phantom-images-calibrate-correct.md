# M. Bastin, P. Armitage (2000)

*On the use of water phantom images to calibrate and correct eddy current induced artefacts in MR diffusion tensor imaging.* — Magnetic Resonance Imaging.

## Claim

The practicalities of using separate phantom calibration data to correct high b-value diffusion tensor imaging data are examined by investigating the stability of these distortion parameters, and hence the eddy currents, with time, and it is found that M, T, and S vary only slowly with time.
