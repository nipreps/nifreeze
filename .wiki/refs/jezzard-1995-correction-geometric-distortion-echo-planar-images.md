# P. Jezzard, R. Balaban (1995)

*Correction for geometric distortion in echo planar images from B0 field variations* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

A method is described for the correction of geometric distortions occurring in echo planar images, caused in large part by static magnetic field inho‐mogeneities, leading to pixel shifts, particularly in the phase encode direction.
