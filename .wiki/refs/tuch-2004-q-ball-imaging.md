# D. S. Tuch (2004)

*Q-ball imaging* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine, 52(6), 1358–1372.

## Claim

Introduces q-ball imaging (QBI): a model-free reconstruction of the diffusion
orientation distribution function (ODF) from a single shell of high-angular-
resolution q-space samples, using the **Funk–Radon transform** to map the
spherical shell of MR signal to the ODF.

## Why relevant

Referenced by [[yeh-2010-generalized-q-sampling-imaging]] as the shell-sampling
q-space method that GQI generalizes: as the diffusion sampling length
$L_\Delta \to \infty$, the GQI $\operatorname{sinc}$ kernel (Eq. 5) approaches a
delta function and the reconstruction reduces to the Funk–Radon transform of QBI
(see [[concept-generalized-q-sampling-imaging]] and
[[q-space-reconstruction-landscape]]). Metadata-level entry (not read in full).
