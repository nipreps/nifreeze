# S. Mohammadi, K. Tabelow, L. Ruthotto, T. Feiweier, J. Polzehl, N. Weiskopf (2015)

*High-resolution diffusion kurtosis imaging at 3T enabled by advanced post-processing* — Frontiers in Neuroscience.

## Claim

The feasibility of high-quality, high-resolution DKI and its potential for delineating highly myelinated fiber pathways in the motor cortex is demonstrated and the combination of REMATCH and msPOAS increased the contrast between gray and white matter in mean kurtosis maps by about 35% and at the same time preserves the original distribution of MK values, whereas standard Gaussian smoothing strongly biases the distribution.
