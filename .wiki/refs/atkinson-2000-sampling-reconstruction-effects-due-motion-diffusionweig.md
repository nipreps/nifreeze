# D. Atkinson, D. Porter, D. Hill, F. Calamante, A. Connelly (2000)

*Sampling and reconstruction effects due to motion in diffusion‐weighted interleaved echo planar imaging* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

Subject motion during diffusion‐weighted interleaved echo‐planar imaging causes k‐space offsets which lead to irregular sampling in the phase‐encode direction, and are shown to lead to a frequent violation of the Nyquist condition.
