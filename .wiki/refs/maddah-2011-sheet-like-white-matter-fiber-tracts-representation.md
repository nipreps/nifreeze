# M. Maddah, James V. Miller, E. Sullivan, A. Pfefferbaum, T. Rohlfing (2011)

*Sheet-Like White Matter Fiber Tracts: Representation, Clustering, and Quantitative Analysis* — International Conference on Medical Image Computing and Computer-Assisted Intervention.

## Claim

An automated and probabilistic method for subject-specific segmentation of sheet-like fiber tracts that provides statistics of diffusion measures by establishing point correspondences on the estimated medial representation of each bundle is introduced.
