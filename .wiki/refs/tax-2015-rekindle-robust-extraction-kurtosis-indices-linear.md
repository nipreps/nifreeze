# C. Tax, W. Otte, M. Viergever, R. Dijkhuizen, A. Leemans (2015)

*REKINDLE: Robust extraction of kurtosis INDices with linear estimation* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

To increase the reliability of diffusion tensor and kurtosis estimates, a robust estimation procedure is proposed for DKI, which is more susceptible to data artifacts than DTI due to its increased model complexity, higher acquisition demands, and longer scanning times.
