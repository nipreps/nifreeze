# K. Embleton, H. Haroon, D. Morris, M. Ralph, G. Parker (2010)

*Distortion correction for diffusion‐weighted MRI tractography and fMRI in the temporal lobes* — Human Brain Mapping.

## Claim

It is demonstrated that dual direction k space traversal with maintained diffusion‐encoding gradient strength and direction results in correction of the great majority of eddy current‐associated distortions in DWI, in addition to those created by variations in magnetic susceptibility.
