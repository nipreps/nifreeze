# Trong-Kha Truong, Nankuei Chen, A. Song (2011)

*Dynamic Correction of Artifacts due to Susceptibility Effects and Time-Varying Eddy Currents in Diffusion Tensor Imaging* — NeuroImage.

## Claim

A novel dynamic B0 mapping and off-resonance correction method that measures the exact spatial, temporal, and diffusion-weighting direction dependence of the susceptibility- and eddy current-induced magnetic fields to effectively and efficiently correct for artifacts caused by both susceptibility effects and time-varying eddy currents, thereby resulting in a high spatial fidelity and accuracy.
