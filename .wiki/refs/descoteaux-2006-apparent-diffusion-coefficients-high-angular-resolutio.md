# M. Descoteaux, E. Angelino, S. Fitzgibbons, R. Deriche (2006)

*Apparent diffusion coefficients from high angular resolution diffusion imaging: Estimation and applications* — Magnetic Resonance in Medicine.

## Claim

This paper presents an extensive study of high‐order models for apparent diffusion coefficient estimation and illustrates some of their applications and proposes a new regularization algorithm based on the Laplace–Beltrami operator.
