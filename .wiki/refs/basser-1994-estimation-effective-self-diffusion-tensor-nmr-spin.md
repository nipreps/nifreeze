# P. Basser, J. Mattiello, D. LeBihan (1994)

*Estimation of the effective self-diffusion tensor from the NMR spin echo.* — Journal of Magnetic Resonance - Series B.

## Claim

Estimation of Deff provides the theoretical basis for a new MRI modality, diffusion tensor imaging, which provides information about tissue microstructure and its physiologic state not contained in scalar quantities such as T1, T2, proton density, or the scalar apparent diffusion constant.
