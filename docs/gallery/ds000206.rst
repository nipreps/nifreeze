=====================
Legacy DTI (ds000206)
=====================

**Source:** `OpenNeuro ds000206 <https://openneuro.org/datasets/ds000206>`__ — ``sub-THP0001/ses-THP0001CCF1/dwi/sub-THP0001_ses-THP0001CCF1_acq-GD31_run-01_dwi.nii.gz``

.. note::

   Traveling human phantom; 30 directions at b=1000 s/mm² (GD31).

Predicted diffusion volumes for the **Legacy DTI (ds000206)** dataset, across every
applicable model in both **LOVO** (leave-one-volume-out, holding out the
predicted orientation) and **single-fit** (fit once on all volumes) modes.
